Love Ambassadors Ministries

Love Ambassadors Ministries (LAM) has grown over the years into a great oak tree, that is carrying, supporting, motivating, mentoring and deploying believers into their God-given ministries.

The set man of the vision, PASTOR DAVID EDWARD IFECHUKWU received the vision of the ministry in December 2002 as a final year student at Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Anambra State Nigeria. This led him to embark on a series of fasting and prayers to seek the mind of God concerning the scope of the ministry He was handing down to him. While continuing the fasting and prayers into January 2003, the name LOVE AMBASSADORS MINISTRIES was clearly handed over to him.

After over thirteen years of ministry, great accomplishments have been recorded in LAM. The year 2011 saw us purchasing our own land, a 10.2 plots, at a cost of about 25 Million Naira which was paid for with surpluses. All praise to the extraordinary grace of God at work in this ministry.

As of 2014, after 11 years of vision fulfilment, over 10,000 persons have been fed, nourished and spiritually discipled in and through LAM. Out of this, over 5,000 persons have been registered members and over 1,000 people have been certified as ministers through our training and leadership institutes (LAM Academy and LALI). We are indeed sowing the seed of Love Sons to the nations!
